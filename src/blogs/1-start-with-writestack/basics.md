# 1. Creating a Queue {#creating-a-queue}

A **queue** is a list of times in which you want to publish your Notes.
A queue makes scheduling 10x easier.

**To create a queue:**
1. Open the **Edit Queue** button in the top right corner of the screen.
   ![Edit Queue Button](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/edit-queue-button.png)
2. Click **Create suggested schedule**.
   ![Create Suggested Schedule](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/create-schedule-button.png)
3. Choose the amount of Notes per day (suggested is 3).
   ![Choose Amount of Notes](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/notes-per-day.png)

Your queue is now ready.

Your followers activity will change over time. Scroll down in the edit queue dialog and you'll see their activity graph.
Click a bar to create a new schedule time for your Notes.

![Followers Activity](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/followers-activity.png)

# 2. Creating Notes {#creating-notes}

You can create Notes in three different ways depending on your workflow.

---

## a. Manually {#manually}

1. Go to the **Notes** page.  
2. Click **New Note**.  
3. Write your content inside the editor (autosave).

Manual Notes give you full control, ideal for raw ideas or quick posts.

---

## b. Notes Generator {#notes-generator}

The Notes Generator helps you create new Notes based on:
- your previous Notes  
- your long-form newsletters  
- files from your computer

**To use it:**
1. Click **Notes Generator**.  
   ![Notes Generator Button](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/notes-generator-button.png)
2. Choose the source.  
   ![Notes Generator Source](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/notes-generator-source.png)
3. Add instructions for the generation (optional).
   ![Notes Generator Instructions](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/notes-generator-instructions.png)
4. Choose length of the Note (suggested: 26-36 words).
5. Choose how many Notes to generate.
   ![Notes Generator Amount](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/notes-generator-options.png)
6. Click **Generate Notes**.
   ![Notes Generator Generate](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/notes-generator-generate.png)

---

## c. WriteStack AI {#writestack-ai}

WriteStack AI is just like ChatGPT, but with your Substack Context.
Perfect for writing notes about general topics that you write about.

**To use it:**
1. Navigate to WriteStack AI on the sidebar.

Pro tip:
You can use Web search, using the *Web search* button in the bottom left of the AI chat.

![Web Search Button](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/web-search-button.png)

## d. Save inspiration from Substack {#save-inspiration-from-substack}

If you find a Note that you liked on Substack, you can easily save it to your Notes drafts.
Click the *lightbulb icon* in the bottom right of the Note.

![Save Inspiration from Substack](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/save-inspiration-from-substack.png)

# 4. Scheduling Notes {#scheduling-notes}

WriteStack gives you **three ways to schedule Notes**, depending on how you prefer to work.

All scheduling requires the WriteStack extension and a Chromium browser, exactly like described in the [Scheduler Guide](/?post=guides-scheduler-guide).

---

## a. Scheduling via the Note Editor {#schedule-in-editor}

1. Open any Note.  
2. Click **Add to Queue** to push it to the next available time slot or click the arrow and **Schedule** to pick a specific time and date.  
   ![Add to Queue Button](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/add-to-queue-button.png)
   ![Schedule Button](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/schedule-button.png)

This is the most precise way. Best when you finish writing and want to schedule immediately.

---

## b. Scheduling via the Shortcut {#schedule-shortcut}

Each draft note has an **Add to Queue** button in the bottom center of it.
   ![Add to Queue Button](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/add-to-queue-shortcut.png)

Great for rapid scheduling.

---

## c. Batch Scheduling {#batch-schedule}

Batch scheduling lets you schedule multiple Notes at once.

1. Go to **Notes â†’ Drafts** and click **Import**.  
2. Click **Batch schedule notes**.  
   ![Batch Schedule Notes Button](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/batch-schedule-button.png)

From there you can either schedule selected Notes or all Notes in the draft list.

WriteStack will attach each Note to the next available time slot in your queue, one after the other.

---

# 5. Reordering Scheduled Notes {#reordering-schedules}

You can reorder your upcoming Notes via **drag and drop**.

1. Open your **Notes â†’ Scheduled Notes**.  
2. Hold and drag any Note to a new position.  
![Reorder Scheduled Notes](https://cdn.writestack.io/writestack-app/blogs/basics/drag-and-drop.png)

This lets you change the schedules easily.
