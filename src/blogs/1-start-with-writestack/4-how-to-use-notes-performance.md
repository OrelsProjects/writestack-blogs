# 3. Repurposing Notes The Right Way {#repurpose}

Repurposing is not recycling.  
Repurposing is *doubling down* on what already works.

### Practical repurposing strategies

**a. Repost with a twist**  
Change:
- the opening hook  
- the angle  
- the length  
- one story or example  
- the CTA  

**b. Turn it into a list**  
If the original was 1 idea ‚Üí rewrite as 3‚Äì5 points.

**c. Expand it into a long Note (with ‚ÄúRead more‚Äù)**  
This increases clicks and dwell time.

**d. Add an image**  
Images dramatically increase revisit count and conversion on Substack Notes.

# 6. Turn This Into a Weekly Routine {#weekly-routine}

**Every Monday:**
1. Sort Notes by **Free Subs**

5. Add to your Queue

**End result:**
